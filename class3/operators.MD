# Operators


## Arithmetic operators

> +, -, *, /, %, **, //


## Assignment operators

> =, +=, -=, *=, /=, %=, **=, //=, &=, |=, ^=


## Comparison operators

> ==, <=, >=, !=, <, >


## Logical operators

> and, or, not


## Identity operators

> is, is not


## Membership operators

> not in, in


## Bitwise operators

> &, |, ^, ~, <<, >>
